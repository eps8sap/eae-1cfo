CFO Consulting Services

CFO consulting delivers targeted financial expertise for specific projects and challenges. Unlike ongoing fractional or outsourced engagements, consulting is scoped around a defined objective with clear deliverables and timelines.

Common Consulting Engagements

Fundraising Preparation

Getting your finances investor-ready takes more than clean books. A CFO consultant builds the financial model, prepares the data room, develops the investor narrative, and coaches your team through due diligence. Typical timeline: 6–12 weeks.

M&A Due Diligence

Whether you are acquiring or being acquired, financial due diligence requires experienced hands. Our consultants analyze target financials, identify risks, model synergies, and support negotiation with data-driven insights.

Financial System Implementation

Migrating from QuickBooks to NetSuite? Implementing a new FP&A tool? A CFO consultant ensures the system meets your financial reporting and analysis needs, manages the transition, and trains your team.

Financial Restructuring

When the business model needs to change, a CFO consultant helps restructure costs, renegotiate debt, optimize the capital structure, and build the financial roadmap for recovery.

Pricing Strategy

Pricing directly impacts margins and growth. A CFO consultant analyzes unit economics, competitive positioning, and customer willingness to pay to develop pricing strategies that maximize profitability.

How Consulting Engagements Are Structured

  1. Scoping: Define the problem, objectives, deliverables, and timeline
  2. Discovery: Data gathering, stakeholder interviews, and current-state assessment
  3. Analysis: Financial modeling, benchmarking, and option development
  4. Recommendations: Detailed findings with actionable recommendations
  5. Implementation Support: Optional hands-on support executing the plan

Consulting vs. Ongoing CFO Services

Choose consulting when you have a specific, time-bound need. Choose a fractional or outsourced engagement when you need ongoing financial leadership. Many clients start with a consulting engagement and transition to an ongoing model as the relationship proves its value.
