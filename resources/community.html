Finance Leaders Community

Connect with other CFOs, controllers, and finance leaders navigating the same challenges you face. Our community provides a space for sharing insights, asking questions, and learning from peers across industries and company stages.

What the Community Offers

Peer Discussions

Engage with finance professionals who understand the challenges of managing financial operations in growing companies. Topics range from technical accounting questions to strategic CFO decisions.

Knowledge Sharing

Members share templates, tools, and approaches that have worked in their organizations. Learn from real implementations, not just theory.

Networking

Build relationships with finance leaders across industries. Many members find that their most valuable professional connections come from peer communities.

Discussion Topics

  • Cash Flow Challenges: Strategies for managing cash in different business models
  • Technology Selection: Real experiences with accounting and FP&A platforms
  • Fundraising Experience: What worked and what did not during capital raises
  • Team Building: Hiring, training, and retaining finance talent
  • Board Management: Effective board communication and reporting
  • Industry-Specific Topics: Financial challenges unique to specific sectors

Community Events

  • Monthly Roundtables: Small-group discussions on timely financial topics
  • Quarterly Meetups: In-person gatherings in major markets (Phoenix, Dallas, Denver, Austin)
  • Annual Summit: Full-day conference with keynotes, workshops, and networking

Join the Community

Our community is open to CFOs, controllers, VP Finance, and senior finance professionals. Whether you work at a startup or a mid-market company, if you are responsible for financial leadership, you belong here.
